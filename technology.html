Technology

The Smart House™ has different technology to offer you to make living in The Smart House™ as smooth and seamless as possible.

The Brain

The brain makes everything work together and be sure that everything runs smoothly by connecting everything together.

Memory Tapes

The Memory Tapes store every request you ask of The Smart House™ and everything that The Smart House™ does on its own to serve you.

Password

This is the start of the list of things that The Smart House has that we call: Self-Protection. Passwords can lock things like rooms, turning on different appliances, or even your front or back door.

Sound Recognition (for pets)

The sound recognition feature can listen for sounds that your pets may make, like barking or meowing, and unlock the doors for them to get into the house.

Window Shutters

Window Shudders can scare away any unwanted animals like deer, rats, snakes, or other animals like bears even. You have the option to choose between window shutters or just window curtains. Continue down the page to see the difference.

Door/Window Curtains

The door/window curtains were mainly meant for the doors, but they can also go on the windows, as you can see in the picture. As stated previously, you have the option to choose between the window shudders or the window curtains. We would recommend the window shudders as they give you more protection and privacy.
