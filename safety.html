Safety

The Smart House™ has a few different safety features in order to keep you safe. Continue scrolling to see what some of the features are! (Please note: These are not the only safety features; this is just the selection we wanted to provide to you.)

Fire Alarms

There are fire alarms in every single room to make loud noises, flash lights, and even tell you if there is a fire anywhere in the house.

Sprinklers

Sprinklers to spray out water from the walls and the ceiling in every room in case of a fire. With the correct placements, the whole room will be covered, so you do not have to worry about that.

Water Rats

Rats that spray out water in case of fire. This method is only used in case the sprinklers don’t work or, for some reason, they don’t cover the whole room.

Green Chemical Robots

Green Chemical Robots to keep you safe by setting different venomous traps.

Green Froth Venomous Snakes

Twenty Green Froth Venomous Snakes that roam around the house to make sure to not have any intruders and make sure that you’re safe.
