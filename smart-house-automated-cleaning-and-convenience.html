The Smart House™ Automated Cleaning/Convenience

The Smart House™ has different ways to make sure it is kept clean.

The different things that it has to keep itself clean are:

  • Automatic dishwasher
  • Robot mice to clean up the dirt in the house and to dust
  • Incinerator to get rid of things that you don't want anymore. (I.E: Trash)
  • Copper scrap rats to take the larger trash to the Incinerator

There is also some convenience things to make your living experience more enjoyable and easy in The Smart House™. (Please note: Some of these items were stated on the homepage for things that The Smart House™ can do for you so you don't have to.)

Those convenience items are:

  • A voice clock to wake you up in the morning, set a schedule for you, give you reminders and do other things that a normal clock can do.
  • A stove to make you breakfast
  • Weather box to let you know of the weather and tell you it's conditions
  • Automatic garage doors
  • Patio tables to set up games, drinks, food, and music
  • Automatic bed warmers that warm according to the temperature
  • Automatic baths to start if you ask it to
  • Automatic garden sprinklers that turn on to keep the garden looking fresh and clean so that way you don't have to
